About Us

Focus on Quailty

Here at TJ Coastal, we have a strong focus on 3 key parts that make our business stand out. Customers, Products & Equipment.

Customer Service and satisfaction
Focusing on upholding a high quality of customer service to ensure customer satisfaction. Stains can be stubborn and not always removed. This will depend on the type & age of the stain & Carpet. We will provide advice on stubborn stains and provide 100% honesty when setting expectations for the end result. 

Environmentally Friendly Products
The Environment is important to us. We set out to use environmentally friendly products on the surfaces we clean. Our suppliers provide us with the data sheets to assist us in ensuring we can uphold this expectation we set out to achieve of being Eco-Friendly. 

Maintained Equipment
The result will always vary, however with maintained equipment we can make sure the result is always the best. We follow machine maintenance schedules and service them regularly to ensure they are running at 100% capacity for the job at Hand. We use quality & trusted brands such as Husqvarna, Rapid Clean & Steam Master.  

Covering these 3 areas provides a quailty end result to the service we provide.

We service all areas across Central Coast of NSW from Umina, Gosford upto Newcastle, Out to Maitland and Surrounds.

We are able to venture into the Mid North Coast, Western Surburbs & Northern Suburbs upon request by appointment only.
