Carpet Cleaning or Steam Cleaning is the use of high pressured hot water forced deep into the fibers of the carpet or upholstery to dislodge the dirt and bacteria buried beneath the surface while simultaneously vacuuming the water removing all the dislodged dirt with it.

Tile & Grout Cleaning is the use of high pressured hot water forced onto the tile & grout while being scrubbed with a rotating brush at speed to dislodge the dirt and bacteria imbedded into the surface while simultaneously vacuuming the water removing all the dislodged dirt with it.
